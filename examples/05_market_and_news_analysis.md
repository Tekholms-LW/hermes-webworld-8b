# Prompt Template: Market & News Analysis

## Goal
Gather and synthesize current market sentiment and news around a topic.

## Prompt
```
Use the simulate_web_action tool to fully plan and simulate the following market research task. Show the complete step-by-step simulated trajectory with the predicted next state after each action.

Task: Analyze current market sentiment and recent news around [TOPIC, e.g. "AI agents" or "Avalanche ecosystem"]. Check recent articles, social media discussions, on-chain metrics, and expert opinions. Simulate a full research session and produce a balanced summary covering: Current Narrative, Key Catalysts, Risks, and Short-term Outlook.
```

## Recommended Settings
- Simulate 14–20 steps
- Ask for balanced (bullish/bearish) view
- Good for: Narrative tracking, macro research, sector analysis

## Example Topics
- AI agents and autonomous systems
- Specific L1 or L2 ecosystems
- Regulatory developments
- New model releases (Qwen, Llama, etc.)
