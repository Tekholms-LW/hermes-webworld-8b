# Prompt Template: DeFi & Yield Research

## Goal
Simulate deep research into current yield opportunities, protocols, and risk factors on a specific chain.

## Prompt
```
Use the simulate_web_action tool to fully plan and simulate the following DeFi research task. Show the complete step-by-step simulated trajectory with the predicted next state after each action.

Task: Research the current best yield farming and lending opportunities on [CHAIN, e.g. "Avalanche"]. Focus on stablecoin yields, new protocols launched in the last 30 days, and any associated risks (smart contract, liquidity, governance). Simulate a full research session and produce a ranked list of the top 5 opportunities with APY, TVL, risk level, and one key risk factor for each.
```

## Recommended Settings
- Simulate 15–22 steps
- Ask for ranked list + risk assessment
- Good for: Yield hunting, protocol discovery, risk analysis

## Example Chains
- Avalanche / Apertum
- Ethereum
- Base
- Solana
- Arbitrum
