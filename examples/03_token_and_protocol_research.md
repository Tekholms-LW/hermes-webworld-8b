# Prompt Template: Token & Protocol Research

## Goal
Perform deep due diligence on a specific token, protocol, or smart contract.

## Prompt
```
Use the simulate_web_action tool to fully plan and simulate the following token research task. Show the complete step-by-step simulated trajectory with the predicted next state after each action.

Task: Conduct a full research session on [TOKEN or PROTOCOL, e.g. "Apertum (APTM)"]. Investigate: current price and market data, recent on-chain activity, contract verification status, liquidity pools, team/background, and any major risks or red flags. Simulate the full investigation and produce a structured research report with sections for Overview, On-Chain Metrics, Risks, and Final Recommendation.
```

## Recommended Settings
- Simulate 18–25 steps
- Ask for structured report format
- Good for: New token launches, protocol audits, investment research

## Example Subjects
- New Avalanche L1 tokens
- DeFi protocol launches
- AI agent tokens
- Layer-2 infrastructure projects
